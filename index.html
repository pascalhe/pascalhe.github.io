Antique Pastries

About Us

At Antique Pastries, we are dedicated to producing the most delectable sweet treats that leave our customers longing for more.

We have a passion for baking top notch pastries: the highest level of quality and attention to detail is at the heart of our production. Our goodies are prepared using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients.

Our inspiration comes from the traditional American pastries that we like to enrich with a touch of Colombian tastes and flavors.

We are a small shop and our goal is to stay small; our size allows us to dedicate a great deal of time and to focus our attention in delivering the most unique experience for each of our loyal customers. This is how we stay true to our craft!
